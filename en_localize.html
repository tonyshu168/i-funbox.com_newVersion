Localization Contributors
Since v0.8, iFunBox is for the user all over the world instead of just for English speakers. Several years ago, we invited some of our users to participate in translating iFunBox to their own language. Without your help, we couldn’t have spread this wonderful program to Apple fans the world over. Today, we are honored to acknowledge all localization contributors of iFunBox2014 below. Thank you very much!

If you want to be the translator of your own language, please send your name, your language name (both in English and in your language) and your personal email to ifunbox.dev@gmail.com. We'll add you to the backup list for future releases.
